The vision of REFAAN is driven by a genuine passion to develop and put in place, a system consisting of all professional artisans in the finishing services sub-sector, both Nigerians and foreigners alike, with a view to training and re-training members transferring of skills to unskilled youths and delivering excellent services to clients at lower cost.

Read this first

At no given time in the annals of Nigeria history has the finishing sub-sector in the building construction industry (Real Estate finishing Sub-Sector) experienced tremendous boost as it is today. With the COVID-19 pandemic, notwithstanding, the sector continues to experience tremendous growth. As we continue to witness advancement in technology, attention has now shifted from physical economy to virtual economy. Different landmark achievements are being recorded in the Real Estate Sector with its attendant growth in the finishing services areas.

The finishing services sector in Nigeria is so huge and largely untapped. This had necessitated the influx of our neighboring west African brothers to come to Nigeria for greener pasture. Ironically, a lot of Nigerian youths have been traveling abroad in search of these greener pastures which are right under their noses, the challenges however are not far-fetched.

These include, Lack of an organized system, lack of awareness and lack of loving support/empowerment. Other challenges with respect to excellent job delivery have to do with some unscrupulous artisans absconding with client’s funds when they have been mobilized and, on the flip side, some clients not making final payment when their jobs have been satisfactorily delivered. This is where REFAAN comes into the picture to address these issues and provide the platform for the robust client’s relationship and trust as well as growth, development and unfoldment of the Nigerian youths.

REFAAN (Real Estate Finishing Artisans Association of Nigeria) is a divinely inspired and duly registered organization in Nigeria with the mandate to liberate and empower the Nigerian youths for economic and financial freedom

Elevating the well-being of artisans, enhancing customers’ satisfaction and developing human capital through mind re-orientation and free transfer of skills.
